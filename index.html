War in Gaza

"Israeli forces had begun transitioning from clearing operations to holding operations in some areas of the northern Gaza Strip. Bombing was at its most intense over the northern part of the Gaza Strip where orange flashes of explosions and black smoke could be seen as morning broke from across the fence in Israel. Planes roared overhead and the booms of air strikes thundered every few seconds, punctuated by rattling gunfire."

Three Things About the Gaza Conflict

    1. About 26% of the Palestinian population is facing hunger and starvation.

    2. An estimate of 60,000 and more counting Palestinians have been injured in Gaza.

    3. 85% of the Palestinian population in Gaza has been displaced, while 2.6% of Israelis have been displaced.
